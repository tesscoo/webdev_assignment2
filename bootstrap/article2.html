The Art of Studying

Why I Enjoy Studying

There's nothing like studying, especially when you know you're understanding the material. As our parents have sent us to a college that costs more than a quarter of a million dollars to finish, it's nice to realize that you're getting something out of your experience. Of course, having fun and partying is nice, but it's also great to be able to go back to your parents and have the ability to prove a complex math problem.
