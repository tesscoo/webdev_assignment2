Good Food, Good People

What do good people and good food have in common?

They both make you feel good. Nothing beats enjoying a great meal; it fills you up and you no longer have that crabby feeling that you get when you're absolutely starving. There is one thing that pairs so well with it, as well, and it isn't a fine wine- it's fabulous company. Eating with your friends also makes you feel amazing. Laughing until your abs are completely exhausted, and smiling until your cheeks hurt is how you know you're around good people.
